Pro Medicus Ltd (ASX: PME) is a Melbourne-based medical IT company. Its core product, Visage 7, provides high-performance PACS/Enterprise Imaging platforms for hospitals and imaging centers worldwide. The company operates with a SaaS + usage-based billing business model, achieving high gross margins and predictable cash flow. It has subsidiaries in the US and Germany, with clients across North America, Europe, and Australia. The short-term strategy focuses on expanding US market penetration; the long-term strategy is to drive innovation in imaging diagnostics through AI and cloud-based platforms.
In FY2024, revenue reached AUD 161.5 million (up 29.3% YoY), net profit was AUD 82.8 million (up 36.5% YoY), and net profit margin remained above 50%. As of May 2025, the market cap approached AUD 28.5 billion.
